Sir Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton

More than just a driver

Here's a timeline of Lewis Hamilton's life so far:

  • 1985 - Born in Hertfordshire, England.
  • 1990 - After dominating the radio-controlled cars at the national BRCA championship, Lewis' father bought him a racing kart. He motivated him to get into racing as long as he did well in school.
  • 1993 - With many wins under his belt, Lewis wins the cadet class championships in karting.
  • 1995 - At 10, Lewis Hamilton becomes the youngest driver to win the British cadet karting driver.
  • 1999 - Races in the Intercontinental A.
  • 2000 - He transfers to Formula A, is named European Champion with maximum points, and quickly rises to Formula Super A by the end of the 2000s.
  • 2001 - He placed fifth in the British Formula Renault Winter series shortly after he joined Manor Motorsport.
  • 2002 - Secures the championship in his third season, which allows him to participate in the British Formula 3 championship.
  • 2003 - Earns fifth place in the championship with Manor Motorsport in the F3 Euro series.
  • 2005 - Lewis goes on to win 15 out of 20 races and wins the F3 Euro series as a driver for ASMotorsports. He is featured in Autosport magazine as number 24 on the Top 50 drivers of 2005. At the end of 2005, he joins ART GrandPrix team to start the following year.
  • 2006 - Lewis wins the GP2 Championship. This allows him to be signed to McLaren to participate in Formula One. He became the first black driver to ever race in F1.
  • 2007 - In his rookie year, he won his first Grand Prix in his sixth race in Canada. Due to his performance, he secured a multi-million-dollar 5-year contract with McLaren. Towards the end of the 2007 season, Lewis earns 2nd place in the 2007 World Driver's Championship.
  • 2008 - Lewis becomes the then-youngest Formula One World Champion with five Grand Prix wins and ten podium finishes.
  • 2012 - Once his contract with McLaren ended, Lewis moved to Mercedes with the Mercedes AMG Petronas Formula One race team.
  • 2013 - Lewis finished fourth in the championship standings in his first season with Mercedes.
  • 2014 - In his second season with Mercedes, with eleven personal wins and nineteen team wins, he secures the F1 Championship.
  • 2015 - Lewis secures a record-breaking deal with Mercedes. A three-year- contract worth over 100 million dollars made him the best-paid sportsman in Britain. At the end of his 2005 season, he won the F1 Championship at the United States Grand Prix in Austin, TX.
  • 2017 - His fourth Formula 1 World Championship title is won In Mexico with two races remaining in the series. He also becomes vegan because he wants to help the earth and have a healthier life.
  • 2018 - Once again, Lewis wins a back-to-back championship in Mexico, making it his fifth world championship. He also Criticizes the F1 organization for its lack of diversity.
  • 2019 - On the last race of the 2019 season in Abu Dhabi, Lewis won his 11th race and secured his sixth world championship title. He also announces he aims to be carbon-neutral by the end of the year.
  • 2020 - Hamilton surpasses the iconic Michael Schumacher in race wins with 100 career wins and pole-positions and ties on world championships with his seventh season win. With his celebrity status, Hamilton increased awareness with the Black Lives Matter movement and took a knee before every race in 2020.
  • 2021 - He created and launched the Mission 44 Foundation program to help bring more individuals from ethically diverse backgrounds into motorsport. He also donated over 2.5 million dollars to health organizations around the world. In honor of a fantastic career in F1 and personal accomplishments, Lewis is knighted by the Prince of Wales.
  • 2022 - Donates more than 20 million dollars to support education and employment and to help the youth.

"You just need to be accepted for who you are and be proud of who you are, and that is what I'm trying to do."

- Sir Lewis Hamilton
